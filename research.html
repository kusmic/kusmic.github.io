Research

Undergraduate Projects

  • Source Extractor Morphometrics: Using prior morphometric calculations of galaxies within CANDELS, we run Source Extractor to see how it compares, via statistical analysis, on accuracy and percision when it comes to calculating galactic morphometry. From our results, we view Source Extractor to be marginally off, but still preserves trends.

    [paper in prep, poster presented as Kusmic, S. & Holwerda, B.W. 2018, AAS Meeting #231, id. 436.09]
  • High-redshift Quantitative Morphology: With what we know of z~8 galaxies, we look to compute quantitative, scale-invariant morphological parameters and see if there exists any sort of trend within this population, and attempt to see if this can be applied to future hunts. We find interesting groupings for these galaxies comparing them to their Gini and asymmetry.

    [paper in prep, poster in prep]
